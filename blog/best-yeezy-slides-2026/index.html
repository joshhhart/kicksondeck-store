Best Yeezy Slides to Buy in 2026 (Every Colorway Ranked)

Kicks on Deck · July 10, 2026 · 5 min read

Slides get overlooked next to the 350 V2 and Foam Runner, but they're the silhouette people actually reach for the most — no laces, no break-in, straight from the shower to the mailbox. If you're buying one pair this year, here's the order that actually makes sense.

1. Onyx — the safest pair you'll ever own

Onyx is all-black, zero contrast, and hides wear better than any other colorway on this list. If you only ever buy one Slide, this is it.

2. Pure (Restock) — the off-white that isn't a hassle

Pure reads clean without the upkeep of true white — a soft, bone-adjacent matte that goes under literally anything.

3. Bone — the warmer everyday neutral

Bone is Pure's sandier cousin and photographs best against tanned skin and summer denim. The connoisseur's neutral pick.

4. Enflame Orange — the one you buy on purpose

Once the neutrals are covered, Enflame Orange is the move — the loudest colorway in the lineup, worn best against all-black or all-white.

What are the best Yeezy Slide colors to start with?

Start with a neutral — Onyx, Pure, or Bone — because it's the pair you'll actually wear on repeat. Once that's covered, add one color-forward pair like Enflame Orange or Ochre for the fits where you want the shoe to do the talking.

Buying your first Slide? Get a neutral. Buying your second? Get the color you keep going back to.
