The Slide is the Yeezy silhouette that does the least talking and the most wearing. One strap, one foam sole, zero laces to think about — which means the colorway is doing all the work. Here's the map, organized by how you'll actually use each one.

## The everyday neutrals

If you're buying your first Slide, it's one of these three. They go under sweatpants, board shorts, or straight out of the shower to the mailbox.

- **[Onyx](/product/onyx-yz-slide/)** — all-black, zero contrast. The safest pair you can own and the one that hides the most wear.
- **[Pure (Restock)](/product/pure-restock-yz-slide/)** — an off-white, bone-adjacent neutral with a soft matte finish. Reads clean without the maintenance headache of true white.
- **[Bone](/product/bone-yz-slide/)** — a warmer, sandier neutral than Pure. The one that photographs best against tanned skin and summer denim.

## The tonal earth run

A step past basic neutral, these lean warm and pair with almost every "quiet luxury" fit in a summer wardrobe.

- **[Desert Sand](/product/desert-sand-yz-slide/)** and **[Earth Brown](/product/earth-brown-yz-slide/)** — sit next to each other on the color wheel; Desert Sand is the lighter tan, Earth Brown the deeper chocolate. Either works with olive, cream, or washed indigo.
- **[Flax](/product/flax-yz-slide/)** — a muted straw-yellow that reads more neutral than it sounds. Underrated pick if you want something slightly different without going bold.
- **[Granite](/product/granite-yz-slide/)** — cool grey-stone, the neutral for people who find Onyx too stark and Bone too warm.

## The color-forward pairs

These are bought on purpose, not as a backup. Summer is when they make the most sense.

- **[Enflame Orange](/product/enflame-orange-yz-slide/)** — the loudest Slide in the lineup, a saturated red-orange that turns a plain fit into a fit. Wear it with all-black or all-white and let it do the work.
- **[Azure](/product/azure-yz-slide/)** and **[Glow Green](/product/glow-green-yz-slide/)** — the two brightest coolers. Azure is a clean sky blue, Glow Green a chartreuse-leaning neon. Both are 2022/2023 restock-era colors, so check the [restock version](/product/glow-green-2022-2023-restock-yz-slide/) if you want the updated foam batch.
- **[Ochre](/product/ochre-yz-slide/)** — mustard-gold, warmer and less neon than Glow Green. The bridge color between the earth tones and the brights.

## The textured/marbled pick

- **[Resin](/product/resin-yz-slide/)** and **[Soot](/product/soot-yz-slide/)** — both sit in a marbled, semi-translucent finish rather than a flat color, similar in spirit to the Foam Runner's MX run. Resin leans amber, Soot leans smoky charcoal. Neither photographs quite like the flat colorways above, which is exactly why collectors reach for them.

## So which one should you get?

Same rule we give for every Yeezy colorway question: your **first** Slide should be a neutral (Onyx, Pure, or Bone) because it's the one you'll actually wear every day. Your **second** should be the color you keep coming back to on this page — for most people that's Enflame Orange or Ochre.
