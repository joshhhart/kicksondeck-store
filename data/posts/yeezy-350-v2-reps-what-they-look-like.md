Search "Yeezy 350 reps" and you'll mostly get stock photos and vague promises. Here's the actual answer: what a good 1:1 350 V2 rep looks like today, pair by pair, so you know what you're actually paying for before you buy.

![the Zebra 350 V2](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0557/4570/8145/products/REP-VERSION-Zebra-Yeezy-Boost-350-V2-Running-Shoes.webp?v=1738037384)

## The short answer

A current top-tier 350 V2 rep gets three things right: the Primeknit weave reads textured (not flat and "sock-like"), the boost sole is bright white and compresses like real boost instead of a denser foam substitute, and the side stripe print sits at the correct weight and placement. Get those three right and the pair is doing its job. We break down all three in more depth in our [rep vs real breakdown](/blog/rep-vs-real-yeezy-350-v2/) — this post is the visual, colorway-by-colorway version of that.

## Zebra

The [Zebra](/product/zebra-yz-boost-350-v2/) is the most-copied colorway for a reason — the red stripe against the black-and-white knit makes any print inconsistency obvious immediately. On a strong pair, the stripe has a faint transparency to it rather than sitting on top of the knit like a sticker.

## Beluga 2.0

The [Beluga 2.0](/product/beluga-2-0-yz-boost-350-v2/) is where the Primeknit weave itself gets scrutinized — the gray-on-gray colorway has nowhere to hide a flat, lifeless knit. This is the pair to look at if you want to judge weave quality specifically, separate from color or print.

![the Black Red 350 V2](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0557/4570/8145/products/REP-VERSION-Black-Red-Yeezy-Boost-350-V2-Running-Shoes.webp?v=1697960628)

## Black Red

An all-dark colorway like [Black Red](/product/black-red-yz-boost-350-v2/) shows up boost quality more than any other pair — there's no busy knit pattern to distract, so a yellowing or dull sole is the first thing anyone notices.

![the Bone 350 V2](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0557/4570/8145/products/REP-VERSION-Bone-Yeezy-Boost-350-V2-Running-Shoes.webp?v=1697960810)

## Bone

Lighter colorways like [Bone](/product/bone-yz-boost-350-v2/) show stitching density fastest — sloppy heel-tab stitching stands out against a light knit far more than it does on a Zebra or Black Red.

## What "1:1" actually means here

We say it on every page: these are replicas, not retail pairs. "1:1" is shorthand for the tier of production, not a claim that they're the real thing — it means the factory is working from the same pattern files and materials sourcing as the highest grade reps on the market, not a budget knockoff. Our [rep terms glossary](/blog/sneaker-rep-terms-glossary/) breaks down what 1:1, OG, UA, PK and QC actually mean if you're new to this.
