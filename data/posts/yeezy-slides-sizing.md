Yeezy Slides are the easiest shoe in the lineup to *wear* and the easiest one to *size wrong*. The question everyone types before they buy is the same: **do Yeezy Slides run big or small?** Short answer — they run big, and they only come in whole sizes, which is the whole reason people get it wrong. Here's the no-return version.


![Editorial sneaker detail for yeezy slides sizing](/assets/blog/yeezy-slides-sizing-2.webp)

## Do Yeezy Slides run big or small?

**They run big.** A slide has no laces and no knit to hug your foot, so a size that's even slightly long slaps at the heel and hangs off the toe. Most people take their Yeezy Slides **a full size down** from their normal sneaker size. If you wear a US 10 in the [350 V2](/collection/350-v2/), you're almost always a **9** in [slides](/collection/slides/).


![Editorial sneaker detail for yeezy slides sizing](/assets/blog/yeezy-slides-sizing-3.webp)

## The whole-size-only problem

Here's the catch that trips everyone up: **Yeezy Slides come in whole sizes only** — no half sizes. So if you're a half size (say US 10.5), you can't just "take your half." Because they run big, the move is to **round down to the nearest whole** — a 10.5 goes to a **10**, not an 11. Sizing up to be safe is how you end up shuffling around in a foam raft.

## The simple rule

- **Whole size, medium feet:** go **one full size down**. Your US 10 is a slide **9**.
- **Half size** (e.g. US 9.5, 10.5): **round down** to the nearest whole. They run big, so the smaller whole is the right call.
- **Wide feet:** go **half a size down** instead of a full size — take the nearest whole *up* from the "one-down" number. Wide feet want the extra width across the strap, and the [Bone](/product/bone-yz-slide/) footbed has room to spare.
- **In between and unsure:** size down. A slightly snug slide breaks in; a big one never gets smaller.

## What about the strap?

The strap feels **tight the first few wears** — that's normal and by design. The foam relaxes and the strap gives a little within a week, so don't size up to escape a snug strap on day one. If the strap is comfortable *immediately* out of the box, the slide is probably too big. The [Core](/product/core-yz-slide/) and [Bone](/product/bone-yz-slide/) are the two that go with everything, so they're the safest first pair to dial your size on.

## How your foot should sit

Dialed-in fit: your foot fills the footbed edge to edge, heel near the back lip, toes just short of the front. A little foam showing at the front is fine — a *lot* means size down. You want the slide to move *with* your foot, not flop a beat behind it.

> TL;DR: Yeezy Slides **run big**. Go **one full size down** (US 10 → slide 9). Half sizes **round down**. Wide feet only drop a **half**. The strap is tight at first and loosens — don't size up to fix it.
