## 1. Cream / Triple White — the do-everything pair

The [Cream 350 V2](/product/cream-yz-boost-350-v2/) goes with literally any fit. No stripe, no statement, just a clean silhouette. The scuffs and patina only make it look better. If you buy one, buy this.


![the Zebra 350 V2](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0557/4570/8145/products/REP-VERSION-Zebra-Yeezy-Boost-350-V2-Running-Shoes.webp?v=1738037384)

## 2. Zebra — the icon tax is worth it

Yes, the [Zebra](/product/zebra-yz-boost-350-v2/) is the "basic" hype pick. It's also the most recognizable sneaker of the decade for a reason — the contrast is perfect and it photographs better than anything else on the list.

## 3. Bone — the slept-on neutral

[Bone](/product/bone-yz-boost-350-v2/) is Cream's warmer cousin. Softer, slightly more interesting, still goes with everything. The connoisseur's neutral.

## 4. Static Reflective — the one detail people notice

Daylight-clean, headlight-loud. The [Static Reflective](/product/static-reflective-yz-boost-350-v2/) is the pair that gets "wait, are those reflective?" every single time.

## 5. Beluga 2.0 — if you want a pop of color

The grey-and-orange Beluga is the move if you're bored of neutrals but not ready for a full statement shoe.
