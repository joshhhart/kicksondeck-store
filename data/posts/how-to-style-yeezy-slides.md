Yeezy Slides went from "house shoe" to a genuine off-duty staple. The trick is treating them like a sneaker, not an afterthought. Six fits that work:


![the Onyx Slide](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0557/4570/8145/products/REP-VERSION-Onyx-Yeezy-Slide-Shoes.webp?v=1697957713)

## 1. The monochrome lounge set

Matching sweat set + a tonal slide (like the blacked-out [Onyx Slide](/product/onyx-yz-slide/)). Effortless, airport-friendly, photographs clean.


![the Bone Slide](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0557/4570/8145/products/REP-VERSION-Bone-Yeezy-Slide-Shoes.webp?v=1697957931)

## 2. Slides + straight denim

Cuff the jeans once so the slide reads intentional. Bone or sand slides against raw denim is the easy win.

## 3. Shorts season

Tailored or knee-length shorts + a neutral [Bone Slide](/product/bone-yz-slide/) is the summer uniform. Skip the ankle socks; this is a no-sock fit.

## 4. The oversized tee drop

Long tee, relaxed shorts underneath, slides. The proportions do the work.

## 5. Socks-and-slides (done right)

It works if the sock is **ribbed and tonal** and the slide is a neutral. Loud sock + loud slide = costume.

## 6. Gym to grocery run

Slides are the fastest "I'm leaving the house" shoe there is. Lean in.

### Quick sizing note

Yeezy Slides run **big** — size **down one** if you're a half size, and consider down one even at whole sizes if you like them snug. They stretch slightly with wear. Full breakdown in the [Yeezy Slides sizing guide](/blog/yeezy-slides-sizing/).

> The whole point of a slide is zero effort. The styling is just making sure "zero effort" still looks deliberate.
